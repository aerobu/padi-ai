11 — Motion
Motion Tokens
Fast and purposeful. Scale-down on press is the signature micro-interaction. No bounce or overshoot.
--dur-fast · 120ms
Button press scale-down. Click the box.
--dur-med · 200ms
Card hover shadow lift. Hover the box.
--dur-slow · 300ms
Section transitions, color fills. Hover.
--ease-standard
cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) — all UI transitions
--ease-out
cubic-bezier(0, 0, 0.2, 1) — entering elements
